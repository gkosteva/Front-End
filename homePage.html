Rules:

At the beginning of the game, all the cards are mixed up and laid in cols and rows, face down on the table. Player starts and turns over two cards: If the cards do not match (it is not a pair), he turns them back over. However, if the two cards match, it is a pair! When the player finds all the pairs, the game is over.
